About Slab City.

Slab City is a netowrk, not a realm. Its based on factions and we are not forgiving. If you run into errors our hands on discord staff team will help you!

Though the servers plugins and insane development, you can play on bedrock with java users!

Events

Slab City hosts a plethora of events frequently! These events are either ran by our organizers or community members like you. Furthermore, we aren't limited to only Shotbow games as we will schedule play-times on different servers and games!

Grow

Share your YouTube videos, Twitch Livestreams, and other content in our bustling media channel! You can also find other media creators to collaborate with and further find enjoyable content from other members within the community.

Community Ran

Slab City is ran by the community for the community! We encourage members to have an active say in how our server is ran, after all, it is just a bunch of gamers who want to have fun! Connect with clan members from Shotbow and start playing!
